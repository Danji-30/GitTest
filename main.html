About us

Welcome to our Agriculture market website, Where agriculture meets innovation! Explore, and Discover the different products of the Filipino local farmers, We are selling their produce products through online market, helping small scale farmers selling their fresh and good quality product to the consumers, this platform spreading awareness and information to the farmers, consumers and etc..!

Mission & Objectives

At Agricultural market website, our mission and objectives aims at fighting against rural depopulation and empowering small farmers who have strong difficulties to sell their products at a fair price and who tend to abandon the field because of low or limited bargaining power, to empower local farmers by providing them with an online market to showcase and sell their products to consumers, who appreciate the hardwork and dedication behind each harvest, we are committted to fostering transparency in the food supply chain, promoting sustainable farming practices, and nurturing a community where everyone can access nutritious, locally grown produce.
